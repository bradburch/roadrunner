What Roadrunner does

Roadrunner shares the nature you saw on each activity with your Strava followers. It pulls the species you logged in your favorite nature apps — eBird and iNaturalist — onto the Strava activity whose time window they fall in, with counts from eBird checklists and names from iNaturalist.

  1. It reads your most recent Strava activities and your latest observations from the nature apps you've linked.
  2. It matches them by overlapping date and time.
  3. For each match, it writes a ”Nature seen during activity” block into the Strava description — existing text is kept, and re-syncing never duplicates it.

Heads up: only eBird checklists with a recorded duration can be matched (incidental observations without a duration are skipped), and iNaturalist observations need a recorded time to place them in an activity.
