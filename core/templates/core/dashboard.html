What Roadrunner does

Roadrunner adds the birds you logged on eBird to the Strava activity you logged them during. When a Strava activity's time window overlaps an eBird checklist, your activity description gets a tidy list of the species and counts from that checklist.

  1. It reads your most recent Strava activities and recent eBird checklists.
  2. It matches them by overlapping date and time.
  3. For each match, it writes a “Birds seen during activity” block into the Strava description (existing text is kept; re-syncing never duplicates it).

Heads up: only eBird checklists with a recorded duration can be matched (incidental observations without a duration are skipped). New activities are also picked up automatically a short time after you save them on Strava — or use Sync now to check your latest activities immediately.
